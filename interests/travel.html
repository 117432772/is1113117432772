My Travel Experience

I thoroughly enjoy travelling. I have visited a number of foreign countries such as Portugal , France and Australia. Throughout my travels, I most enjoyed seeing the different kinds of scenery. The Sydney Opera house and the Eiffel Tower are the epitome of architectural beauty and I hope to get a chance to see them again.

My Future Travel Plans

I hope to visit New York within the next few years. I love the atmosphere of the city portrayed on television and I would love to visit the various monuments around the area also.

Why Travel is Important

In my opinion, travel experience is essential in life. It allows the possibility for us to have a wider perspective of the world and other cultures.This is a skill that could prove to be useful in the workplace and help strengthen career opportunities as we can be more understanding of others and thus improve our interpersonal skills.
